---DESIGN CONSIDERATIONS---
LTC7003 should be operated from RUN pin, not INP.
LTC7003 should have a resistor at the gate of the mosfet it's controlling who's value can be decided post-manufacture of the board
LTC7003 should have SNS+ shorted to the high side of its MOSFET
LTC7003 should have SNS- connected (via a resistor?) to the high side of its MOSFET
LTC7003 should have a reverse-biased shottky diode connected to the TS pin in order to handle inductive loads
LTC7003 should have a minimum 0.1uF capacitor between VIN and GND
LTC7003 should have a decoupling capacitor of minimum 1.0uF between VCC and GND
LTC7003 should have FAULT pulled up to VCC
LTC7003 should have OVLO tied to GND if not in use
LTC7003 should have a minimum 0.1uF capacitor tied between BST and GND



---LAYOUT CONSIDERATIONS---
Screw terminals should be inset from the edge of the board to allow for a "wire clamp" on the final board
LTC7003 SNS connections should be Kelvin connected to each other
LTC7003 capacitor between BST and TS should be kept close to the board
LTC7003 TS trace should be low resistance (wide and short)
