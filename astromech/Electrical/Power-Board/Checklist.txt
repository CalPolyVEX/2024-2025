---DESIGN CONSIDERATIONS---
LTC7003 should be operated from RUN pin, not INP.
LTC7003 should have a resistor at the gate of the mosfet it's controlling who's value can be decided post-manufacture of the board
LTC7003 should have SNS+ shorted to the high side of its MOSFET
LTC7003 should have SNS- connected (via a resistor?) to the high side of its MOSFET
LTC7003 should have a reverse-biased diode connected to the TS pin in order to handle inductive loads

---LAYOUT CONSIDERATIONS---
Screw terminals should be inset from the edge of the board to allow for a "wire clamp" on the final board
LTC7003 SNS connections should be Kelvin Connections
